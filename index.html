Battle Selection

Single Battle

Single Battle (Japanese: シングルバトル Single Battle) is a battle mode introduced in Generation I for battles through the Pokémon Cable Club. As the name suggests, in Single Battle mode, players will battle with only one Pokémon sent out at any one time.

Double Battle

A Double Battle (Japanese: ダブルバトル Double Battle), also known as a two-on-two battle, is a Pokémon battle with each side featuring two Pokémon at once. A Double Battle with two Trainers per side who each control one Pokémon is called a Multi Battle.

Multi Battle

A Multi Battle (Japanese: マルチバトル Multi Battle), referred to as a Tag Battle (Japanese: タッグバトル Tag Battle) in the anime, is a type of Double Battle in which each of the four Pokémon is controlled by a separate Pokémon Trainer.
